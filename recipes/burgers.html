my burger recipe

ingredients

  • pre-made burger patties or ground beef
  • onion powder
  • black pepper
  • herbs seasoning
  • all purpose seaoning
  • cheese
  • lettuce
  • ketchup
  • mayo
  • burger buns

    • how to make a burger

  • 1. make sure to thaw out your meat and prepare your seasonings
  • i measure my seasonings by eye, but i use not too much onion powder. a little bit of black pepper. a lot of all purpose seasoning. a little bit of seasoned salt (you don't need to add this) and a little bit of onion powder.
  • after seasoning form your patties by forming them in a shape of a ball in your hand. press it down to make the burger patties.
  • after that pan fry them or put them in the oven until cooked.
  • toast your bread and make the sauce using ketchup and mayo. put the ketchup and mayo in a container and mix together
  • spread sauce on burger buns and add whatever you want. you could add lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, pickles, etc.

    • congrats you have a burger enjoy... ___〆(・∀・)
