my cookies recipe

ingredients for cookies

  • melted butter
  • brown sugar
  • salt
  • baking soda
  • egg yolk
  • vanilla extract
  • flour
  • chocalate chips
  • cornstarch

    • how to make cookies?

  • preheat your oven to 375 degrees
  • mix together butter, sugar, vanilla, salt, baking powder, and bakingsoda until well combined
  • add the egg and beat it smooth
  • add the flour and if your using chips add them in after
  • put the cookies onto prepared baking sheets spacing them 2 inches apart
  • bake for 10 to 14 minutes
  • remove the cookies and enjoy
